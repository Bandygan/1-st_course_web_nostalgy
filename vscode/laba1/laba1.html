    Big Floppa or simply Floppa refers to a series of ironic memes based on photographs of a caracal named Gregory, as well as photographs of other medium-sized and small cats such as lynxes and servals. Originally circulated among ironic Instagram meme accounts starting in late December 2019, the meme gained more widespread popularity online starting in May 2020.
